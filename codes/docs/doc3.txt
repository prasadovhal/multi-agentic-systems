Doc3: Reimbursement Policy

Reimbursement is processed after submitting hospital bills and discharge summary. The insurer reimburses based on sum insured and sub-limits. Room rent limits affect overall claim amount. Co-payment clauses may reduce final payout. Non-medical expenses are excluded. Claims are processed within 15–30 days after verification. Cashless claims are preferred but reimbursement is available for non-network hospitals.
